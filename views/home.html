AI-based issue detection

Upload photos and let the platform identify civic problems automatically.

  • Image classification for issue type
  • Auto mapping to canonical categories
  • Faster reporting and consistency
📍

GPS-based problem reporting

Capture exact coordinates to improve accuracy and dispatch.

  • Browser-based location capture
  • Precise latitude and longitude storage
  • Mobile-friendly reporting flow

Complaint tracking system

Track civic issues from submission to resolution with clear status updates.

  • Ticket IDs and progress tracking
  • State updates for each complaint
  • Improved accountability

  • Public issue visibility
  • Map-driven civic transparency
  • Community trust and awareness

  • Vote on reported issues
  • Surface important civic problems
  • Community-powered verification

Citizen reward system

  • Points for reports
  • Recognition for participation
  • Motivation for civic action
